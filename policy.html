Rofinds Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Rofinds is a Discord bot created by @sliturthroatatdusk. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

Rofinds only collects minimal information required to operate commands, such as:

  • Your Discord username and ID (to display user info)
  • Data related to commands you use (e.g., /avatar)

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide bot functionality. This may include:

  • Displaying user information
  • Processing command requests

4. Data Retention

We do not store data permanently. Any temporary data collected is deleted automatically after use.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the bot after updates constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

7. Contact
